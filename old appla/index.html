Mentoring

Intro Call 

Book an informal call to discuss your motivation and current situation.
The Intro Call is free of charge as we additionally have to ensure it's also a chemistry fit.                    

Ongoing Mentoring

Typically we have a session every second week, we can also find a different rhytmn (e.g. weekly or monthly) if this is more fitting.
We align on a clear goal for the Mentoring and measure progress against it.
Additionally we can dive into problems or opportunities which you are currently facing.
If wanted we can also have some joint sessions together with your direct manager to get approval and buy-in towards your goals.     

Company Paid of Self-Funded 

A lof of my Mentees get their Mentoring paid fully or partially by their company, e.g. through their dedicated Learning & Development budget. Some Mentees are self-funded. Check with your employer whether they can contribute to your Mentoring expenses as it is in your mutual interest that you grow.
