Mentoring

How does it work?

Intro Call

Arrange a no-cost introductory call to discuss your aspirations and situation. It's essential for us to have a good chemistry fit, ensuring a mutually enjoyable and productive mentorship.

Ongoing Mentoring

Sessions are generally every other week, but we can tailor the frequency to your needs (weekly/monthly). We will establish clear mentoring goals and monitor your progress, focusing on current challenges and opportunities. If desired, we can include joint sessions with your direct manager for goal alignment and support.

Payment Options

Many mentees have their mentorship funded (fully or partially) by their employer, typically through Learning & Development budgets. Self-funding is also an option. It's worth discussing with your employer about contributing to your mentorship, as it benefits both parties.

Who Benefits from Mentoring?

  • Data Analyst at any level
  • Lead / Manager / Head
  • Career Change into Data
  • Founders

Consulting

How does it work?

Consulting Introduction

Arrange an introductory call to dive into your consulting needs. Whether it's about building a data department, developing a data strategy, hiring, technical or tool choices, or any other data-related challenges, let's discuss how I can assist you.

Consulting Sessions

Tailored to fit your schedule and needs, our sessions can range from a few hours a week over small workshops to short-term projects. We'll focus on establishing and achieving specific goals in various areas of data analytics, ensuring practical solutions and strategic insights for your unique challenges.

Ideal for:

  • Organizations developing or building their data capabilities
  • Leaders seeking guidance in data strategy and analytics
  • Teams looking for expertise in technical or tool selection
  • Business undergoing digital transformation
  • Start-ups requiring scalable data frameworks for rapid growth
  • Marketing departments wanting to level up their measurement capabilities
