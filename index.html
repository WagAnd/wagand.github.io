Mentoring

It's Best Simple & Useful

Intro Call

Book an informal call free of charge to discuss your motivation and current situation. We additionally check that it's also a chemistry fit between the two of us: Both of us have to enjoy the Mentorship    

Ongoing Mentoring

Typically we have a session every second week, we can also find a different rhytmn (e.g. weekly or monthly) if this is more fitting. We align on a clear goal for the Mentoring and measure progress against it. Additionally we can dive into problems or opportunities which you are currently facing. If wanted we can also have some joint sessions together with your direct manager to get approval and buy-in towards your goals.

Company Paid of Self-Funded

A lof of my Mentees get their Mentorship paid (fully or partially) by their company, e.g. through their dedicated Learning & Development budget. Some Mentees are self-funded. Check with your employer whether they can contribute to your Mentoring expenses as it is in your mutual interest that you grow.
